How it works

Sign Up or Login with your details to send or order a book free of cost*

Enter the details of the book you want to give off by selecting "Send Book" in the menu

We will collect the book (in good condition) from you

Check the list of books on Old Book Shelf and order a book. You can order a book only after we receive the book you sent. It is mandatory to send us a novel if you want one from our shelf.

Receive the book you ordered. Also get discount coupons as bonus for purchasing latest novels from online stores
